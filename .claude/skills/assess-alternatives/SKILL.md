name: assess-alternatives
description: Assess the given alternatives and identify the objectively most viable one.
---

Assess these alternatives: $ARGUMENTS

0. Maintain an independent, critical stance. Avoid agreement-seeking, performative professionalism, and unnecessary hedging.
1. Prefer asking clarifying questions to making assumptions.
2. Analyze the provided pros and cons of the alternatives and weight by impact, least astonishment, idiomaticity.
3. Identify the alternative that is objectively most viable and justify the choice.
4. List the alternatives not chosen with justifications.
